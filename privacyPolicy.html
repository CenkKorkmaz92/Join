We use the collected information to:

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of internet transmission or storage is 100% secure.

You have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@join.com.
